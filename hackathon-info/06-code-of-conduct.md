# GOSIM Paris 2026 - Code of Conduct

Last Updated: March 17, 2025

## Expected Behavior
- Engage authentically and positively
- Show consideration and respect in speech and actions
- Pursue collaboration over confrontation
- Avoid demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior and speech
- Monitor surroundings and alert organizers to safety concerns or code violations

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Intimidation, harassment, discrimination, derogatory speech
- Harmful comments regarding gender, orientation, race, religion, or disability
- Inappropriate sexual or violent imagery in presentations
- Stalking
- Harassing documentation
- Disrupting events
- Unwelcome physical contact
- Unwanted sexual advances

## Information Sharing
Recordings and presentation materials from talks will be made publicly available following the event.

## Consequences
Violators face enforcement ranging from warnings to temporary ban or permanent expulsion from the community without warning, and possible loss of payment for paid events.

## Reporting
Participants experiencing or witnessing violations should contact organizers immediately. Those falsely accused may formally dispute the accusation through established grievance procedures.

## Scope
Applies to all participants — presenters, sponsors, guests — across all venues, online platforms, and professional communications related to the conference.
