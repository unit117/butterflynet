# GOSIM Paris 2026 - Overview

## Event
- **Name:** GOSIM Paris 2026 — "The Agentic AI Convergence"
- **Dates:** May 4-6, 2026 (May 4 = setup/workshops, May 5-6 = main event)
- **Hours:** 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM both main days
- **Location:** STATION F, 5 Parvis Alan Turing, 75013 Paris, France
- **Hosts:** GOSIM Foundation, CSDN, 1ms.ai
- **Theme:** Agentic AI, open source models, agent frameworks, edge deployment
- **Note:** Early bird 30% discount ends April 13 (today)

## The Hackathon
- **Name:** GOSIM Agentic Hackathon 2026
- **Prize Pool:** $10,000 across 5 awards (confirmed on create.gosim.org as of 2026-04-13). Note: earlier web search results claimed €200,000 — this appears inaccurate.
- **Capacity:** ~111 spots total; as of 2026-04-13: 13 teams, 26 registered, 85 spots remaining
- **Registration:** https://create.gosim.org/ — **Free to participate**
- **Eligibility:** In-person only at STATION F. Open to AI engineers, open-source contributors, startup founders, researchers, indie developers, students, and creators.
- **Team Size:** 1–3 people. Can register solo and find teammates during Day 1 team formation.
- **Focus:** Design, develop, and deploy agentic systems that can reason, plan, and act — pushing the boundaries of what open-source AI agents can accomplish in real-world scenarios

### Hackathon Themes (7 Tracks)
1. **Agents Meet Apps** — Reimagine a mainstream app (Twitter, WeChat, TikTok, Spotify, Gmail, etc.) with an agent-first approach.
2. **Claws and Octos** — Ecosystem co-creation around OpenClaw (open-source personal AI assistant) and Octos (OS layer for OpenClaw).
3. **Human–Agent Interaction** — Redefine HCI for the agentic era: intent understanding, context perception, proactive action.
4. **Education** — Reimagine learning with agentic AI.
5. **Content Remixing** — Combine AI capabilities across modalities (text, music, images, video, narration, simulation).
6. **Productivity** — Agents that understand business context, autonomously execute multi-step tasks, coordinate across systems.
7. **Agents with Voices** — Voice-enabled agents combined with smart hardware and IoT devices.

Other models/frameworks are also permitted.

### Submission Requirements
- Working demo
- Open-source GitHub repository
- Short slide deck
- Flash Demo Presentation + Live Q&A

### Judging Criteria (each 20%)
1. **Innovation** — Originality and novelty of the idea
2. **Technical Depth** — Architecture, code quality, technical complexity
3. **Completeness** — Working demo, functional completeness
4. **Practicality** — Real-world problem-solving potential
5. **Presentation** — Clarity and persuasiveness of the demo
