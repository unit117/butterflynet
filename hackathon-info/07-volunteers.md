# GOSIM Paris 2026 - Volunteer Info

## Why Volunteer
- Work alongside world-class open-source maintainers, AI researchers, and robotics engineers
- Network with speakers and attendees
- Gain hands-on event experience
- Free conference access
- Exclusive volunteer merchandise (T-shirt)

## Roles
- Registration & check-in
- Speaker and session support (A/V assistance)
- Wayfinding and attendee guidance
- Stage coordination across tracks
- Community engagement for networking activities

## Requirements
- Passionate about open source
- Available during event dates (May 5-6, some roles may need May 4 for setup)
- Must commit to at least one full day
- No prior event experience required — training provided
- Bilingual volunteers (English/French or additional languages) especially welcome
