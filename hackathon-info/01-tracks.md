# GOSIM Paris 2026 - Conference Tracks

## 5 Core Tracks (per main site; some sources list 6)

### AA01 - Agentic AI Summit
The strategy layer of the agentic wave: protocols, software patterns, tooling, and business models. Covers industrial implementation of AI agents, enterprise AI, and global collaboration.

### OS02 - Open Source Robotics
Hardware integration with agentic systems. Embodied intelligence and robotics technologies. Features OpenArm — a fully open-source 7 DoF humanoid robot arm. Sessions on robot benchmarks, 3D-printed humanoids, surgical AI, visual navigation, embodied learning, and expressive robotics.

### AA03 - Agentic AI on Edge
On-device AI deployment and inference. Sessions on llama.cpp implementation, WebNN architecture, Rust-based inference, and MiniCPM on-device implementations.

### AO04 - Agentic OS & App
Multi-agent coordination and operating systems. Focus on building AI applications with Rust, StrongDM, CopilotKit. Sessions on multi-agent systems and distributed scaling.

### OS05 - Open Source Model
Language, vision, audio, and video model advances. Coverage of linear attention mechanisms, GLM models, world models, sparse LLMs, and multimodal model training. Includes SmolLM3 training practices at scale.

### Agentic App (may be merged into AO04)
Agent application design and deployment. Some sources list this as a separate 6th track; others fold it into AO04 - Agentic OS & App.

## Workshops

### WS-01: SGLang Workshop
High-performance inference framework for LLMs and multimodal models. Structured generation, RadixAttention. (7 sessions on Day 2)

### WS-02: vLLM Workshop
Industry-leading inference framework. Paged attention, continuous batching. (3 sessions on Day 1)

### WS-03: Own Your Data Science and AI Workshop
Full-day workshop on Day 1. Topics include TabICLv2, GPU acceleration for ML, production data pipelines, scikit-learn optimization, sovereign AI development. (18 sessions)

### WS-04: Build Your Own Agentic App Workshop
Hands-on workshop spanning Day 2. (4 sessions)

## Special Events

### GOSIM Spotlight 2026
Live demo competition with 15-20 selected projects. Selected creators receive full travel sponsorships to fly to Paris and pitch directly to global top-tier investors and tech visionaries.

### Robotics Hackathon
Hands-on hackathon featuring OpenArm 7 DoF humanoid robot arm. Teams of 3-5 apply state-of-the-art models on physical hardware to solve challenges in robotic grasping and manipulation.

### GOSIM Agentic Hackathon
The main hackathon: design autonomous AI agents for real-world scenarios. €200k prize pool, 8,000+ participants (hybrid).
